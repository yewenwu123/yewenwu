FAQ

Questions clients may ask before they enquire

Do you sell directly online like a normal shop?

No. This site is designed for enquiry first. Buyers contact you, explain what they need, and then you decide whether to recommend or source products.

Can Australian retailers use this service?

Yes. Independent optical stores, online sellers, and boutique retailers can use the consultation form to discuss styles, quantities, and repeat purchasing needs.

Can individuals also enquire?

Yes. The form supports both personal clients and businesses, so you can serve either type without changing the website structure.
